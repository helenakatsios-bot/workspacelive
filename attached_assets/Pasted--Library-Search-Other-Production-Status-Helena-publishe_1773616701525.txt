Order Manager — Internal Staff Tool for Purax Pty Ltd

This is a staff-only, internal production management system — not customer-facing at all. Customers never see or interact with it. It's used by the Purax warehouse/production team to manage and process customer orders from start to finish.

What it does:

Staff upload customer order PDFs (received from wholesale clients like interior designers, hotels, furniture stores, etc.). The app uses AI to automatically read the PDF and pull out the customer name, address, order number, products ordered, and total amount.

From there, staff work through each order:

Mark orders as In Progress when work begins, and Completed when done
Capture completion photos and collect a digital signature from the customer or staff member
Print packing lists and track whether labels have been printed and inserts are ready
Flag orders as Urgent or put them On Hold (Do Not Start) if awaiting payment
Add notes and annotate PDFs or photos directly in the app
Once an order is complete, an admin can send it to Xero (the accounting software) to create a draft invoice automatically, with the order files attached. After invoicing, the system sends a notification to Millie (the CRM) with the order and customer details.

Other features:

Production List — a daily summary page that groups all active orders by product type (e.g. 80% Goose Down, Hungarian Goose Down, Duck Down, Inserts) so the team can see at a glance what needs to be made
Stock Inventory — tracks raw materials, with low-stock alerts sent by email
Worker Analytics — tracks how long each staff member takes to complete orders
Portal-based access — the app has two separate portals: one for "Quilts & Pillows" orders and one for "Inserts" orders, so different staff only see their relevant orders
In short: it's a private internal operations tool that bridges the gap between receiving a customer order PDF and getting a Xero invoice sent — handling everything in between.
